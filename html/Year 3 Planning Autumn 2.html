Autumn 2

Year 3 Planning

To use research to inform knowledge of stable structures.

To use a design brief to evaluate how stable their product is, using this to suggest a design improvement.

To use knowledge of construction and joining materials to test mock ups.

To apply their knowledge of mock-up evaluation in their designs.

To know how to create a hidden join.

To communicate their designs using labelled sketches and ordered steps.

To select suitable construction to create a free-standing structure.

To know how to score using scissors.

To know that the shape and size of a structure’s base affects its stability.

To know that a buttress is placed against a wall to make a structure more stable.
