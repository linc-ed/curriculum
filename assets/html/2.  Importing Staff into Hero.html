Importing Staff into Hero

As part of your Hero Implementation document, your school would have received a Staff Spreadsheet template.   Ensure this has been completed for all staff members who will require access to Hero. 

 

NB:  If your school has an earlier version of the spreadsheet with additional columns, please remove all columns besides those shown below.

STEP 1:  PREPARE THE STAFF IMPORT FILE

 

1. Enter the details of all your staff members into the Staff Spreadsheet template supplied.

 

Your Staff Spreadsheet should look like this:

 

Please ensure that:

  1. The column headings are the same as those above.
  2. All staff have a unique email address.
  3. Your Class names match those in your Legacy SMS.  (classes can be renamed in Hero if required)
  4. Your Classes relate to the same year as the student data.  e.g.  If your student data will be in 2020 at the time of import, your classes will need to be your 2020 classes.
  5. The Team relates to the Class i.e. Learning Hub/Syndicate etc

 

2. Save your staff spreadsheet as a .csv file in your data transfer folder.

 

STEP 2: IMPORT DATA INTO HERO

 

1.  In HERO click Import Data in the Administration Menu.

 

 

 

2.  Click Staff Members.

3.  Select your .csv file to upload either by dragging it into the Drop here box or by clicking browse to find it on your computer.  If you are unable to find your file, it may be because you have not saved it as a .csv file.  Click NEXT.

 

4.  The column headings will map automatically.

 

 

 

5.  Click NEXT.

 

6.  A preview of your data will be displayed to check. If all required fields look correct (i.e. First Name, Last name, Gender, Main Role & Email Address), click IMPORT, otherwise click BACK.

 

 

 

Your staff will now be imported into Hero.  If any errors are reported that you don’t understand, snapshot the error message and contact the Hero helpdesk.  NB:  Any staff members previously created will be skipped by the import.

 

Classes will not be created at this point but are needed for a later process.

 

STEP 3: UPDATE STAFF PERMISSIONS

 

To amend staff access rights i.e. Administrator access.

 

1.  Click Staff Members in the Administration Menu.

 

 

2.  Click the Menu icon for the staff member you wish to change.

 

 

3.  Click ADMIN PAGE and adjust the sliders accordingly.
